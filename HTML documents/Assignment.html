Golden Retriever were first bred from Scotland in the mid 19th century. This breed was developed as a hunting breed. They first came to North America in the 1920's where they became popular for their excellent temperament and beautiful appearance. In 1925, the AKC (American Kennel Club) registered the golden retriever as a breed.
